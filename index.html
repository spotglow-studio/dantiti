Born name

Dantiti is a Nigerian-born artist whose sound flows between Afrobeats, Afrofusion, rap, and R&B. Raised in Benin City, he first fell for music as a kid rapping along to Notorious B.I.G. Dreaming of becoming a DJ while the rest of the world was still catching up to what Lagos was building.

That passion followed him from Nigeria to Ghana and later to the UK, where he studied Marketing at the University of Wolverhampton. Not to leave music behind, but to understand the business well enough to own it fully.

In 2017, he founded iDope Records, a family-run label built around independence, creative control, and integrity. He began shaping his production sound while collaborating with Princetonbeatz in Lagos, a partnership that gave him the tools to tell his own story.

His music carries an easy optimism. The feeling that wherever life leads, there is always a rhythm to move to. He writes about love, resilience, and balance, themes that feel universal, told through a sound that is entirely his own.
